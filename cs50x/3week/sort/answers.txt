sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: Because it takes significantly less time sorting already sorted list than random and reversed. Which clearly reflects quality of bubble sort.

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: Because it takes less time sorting random and reversed list than the other two sorts. And also it is not significantly better at sorting already sorted list like sort1.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: Because it takes almost same time sorting all three type of list. Which is the quality of selection sort.
